Legal notice

Participation in AU Cultur, an initiative developed by the research unit Econcult recognised by the Universitat de València, is voluntary. These web application and questionnaire is a prototype being tested by the Universitat de València and the Universidad Politécnica de Valencia. It is part of a research project, with European funding (H2020 Grant Agreement 870935) that aims to deepen the way to measure the social impacts of culture.

The project is non-profit and its results, through aggregated data, will be distributed, where appropriate, in open access becoming available to public and non-profit organisations whom consider that with this information can improve the social value of their programmes and actions. The person responsible for the data is the Cultural Economics Research Unit of the University of Valencia, in the person of its coordinator Pau Rausell Köster. The rights of access, rectification, suppression, limitation, portability and opposition are guaranteed at any time. These rights may be exercised by sending a request by post to econcult@uv.es.

The microdata will only be accessible to researchers of the Universitat de València with a commitment of confidentiality. The data obtained through the form will never be transferred to third parties and will only be presented publicly and within the objective of the research in an aggregate form without it being possible either directly or indirectly to identify the specific individuals to whom the data correspond. The data will be kept in digital devices owned by the Universitat de València, with the security protocols and procedures in force at the Universitat de València. Data subjects may also withdraw their consent and request the deletion of their data at any time and in certain circumstances, as regulated by the GDPR 2016/679.
